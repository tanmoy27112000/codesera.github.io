When you play a game in the console, where you get feedback according to your game-play. Haptic feedback technology is on behind them. This technology works in similar to 4D technology. Here you can get a more realistic feel, it can feel you that you are in this game, video or whatever you saw. Just think that you see anything in AR effect and get feedback like a real spot, it can be your dream-land. You will live in own world.

Now many console came with this technology, it will vibrate according game-play.

How Haptic feedback works:

Here you have to wear clothes or gadgets. Then there should have a mechanism which gave you feedback by giving some pressure with some spacial angle. This technique will be based on AI. It has to train all things that which specific angle give which feels. As you can see that on the iPhone, actually the home button doesn’t press but when you press on that it gave a feeling that as you press this button. This module calls “Taptic Engine“. You can see iPad track-pad doesn’t move anywhere but still, you thought that it is pressed. Apple 3D touch act as the same technique. All this feeling gave taptic module.

Already we develop many jackets, gloves etc to give you a real feeling that you are in this game. All feeling will be given by specific feel vibration!

Usage of haptic technology:

  • Game-play: When you playing a game, it will make this more realistic by some realistic touch feedback.
  • Medical surgery: When doctors make a remote surgery, it will be better when doctors can feel everything and this will be more accurate. It will feel a doctor that they are on the spot or involved.
  • Reading: If any student sees a 3D model through VR and can feel this object, it will be more memorable or can understand more better.

As this all things will be realistic and you can feel those. And also by this haptic technology, you can make your weekend, tourist-spot in your home and can live in your own dream. Let me know your query in comment bellow.
