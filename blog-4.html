Technology has undergone drastic change over the decades. People have more power in their smartphones than at home. All thanks to technology. Artificial intelligence is an amazing concept in science since decades however; in the recent years break through have been made in machine learning. The breakthrough has been caused using neural networks. The processes of actual neurons are mimicked by the neural networks. Scientists believe that artificial intelligence future is bright in the coming years.

Transportation:

The transportation has been automated and shelf driving cars are available in the market. The driver is available at the wheel for safety purposes. There are still drawbacks in the technology and will take time for public acceptance.

Deployment of dangerous jobs:

There are certain jobs which are difficult and cannot be handled easily by the human beings. These jobs may create life threat to the human beings. Robots are taking over such difficult jobs. These robots are technically treated as drones which are used as the alternatives for human beings. These robots are used as the physical counterparts for defusing bombs which are controlled by humans instead of artificial intelligence.

Other difficult jobs are also being taken into consideration which include wielding. Wielding produce toxic substance, unbearable noise and intense heat. Such jobs can be outsourced to robots. Researchers and scientists are experimenting on artificial intelligence which provide hope for the future of robotics and artificial intelligence. Robots also help to solve the climatic changes. Robots will have the capability to access more data when compared to human beings. The days may arise in future where the artificial intelligence utilizes the trends identified to achieve solutions for the major issues across the globe.

The days are not too long where humans may interact with the artificial intelligence on daily basis. Artificial intelligence will be an everyday activity and be a part of human life soon.
